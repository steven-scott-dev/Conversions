What should I expect at my first visit?

You will be welcomed in, asked a few quick questions about your goals, and guided into a treatment that fits what you need. The process is simple, calm, and never rushed.

Do you offer couples or group bookings?

Yes. Couples sessions and small group bookings are available by request, based on room and staff availability.

How do I choose the right treatment?

Pick the service that sounds closest to your goal and we will help fine-tune it. Many first-time clients start with a custom facial or Swedish massage.

Do I need to book ahead?

Yes, especially for evenings, weekends, and new client specials. Booking ahead gives you the best chance of getting the time you want.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for changes or cancellations so that appointment time can be offered to another client.
